You Hired an Architect. Now What?

  3. Weekly Async Check-Ins: No syncs unless critical. Use Loom videos, Slack updates, and monthly scorecards.
  4. 90-Day Revenue Milestone: Tie engagement to 1 clear KPI: CAC reduction, qualified lead growth, or positioning wins.
  5. Scale or Exit: After 90 days, either hire a full-time Director or renew fractional engagement for deeper strategic work.
